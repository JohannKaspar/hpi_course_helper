About HPI Course Guide

Project Overview

The HPI Course Guide was initiated by Johann Lieberwirth on November 9, 2023, with the objective of improving the way students at Hasso-Plattner-Institute access academic course information. This application provides a centralized platform for easy navigation and management of course-related details.

Challenge and Solution

With the challenge of fragmented course information across multiple platforms, the HPI Course Guide offers a unified solution. It brings together essential details such as course schedules, instructor information, and evaluations, facilitating easier course selection and management.

Key Features

  • Streamlined course search and discovery.
  • Integration of course evaluation data.
  • Personalized course recommendations.
  • Automated study plan creation.
